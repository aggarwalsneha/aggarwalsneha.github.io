Pizza

Take the dough out and roll it into a circle.Puff out the edge of the dough so the crust is thicker at the edge.Pre-heat oven to 250-260 degrees C. Put on the pizza sauce, cheese, and toppings.Put the pizza on the pizza stone in the oven. Bake for 30 minutes or until pizza is crispy and golden brown.Serve and enjoy!

Noodles

Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and 3 spring onions and cook for 2-3 minutes. Add the carrots and peas, before stirring in the brown sugar and soy sauce. Add the noodles and mix together for another couple of minutes. Season with salt and pepper, then slice remaining spring onion and top to serve. Enjoy!
